Our Story

Maqsood Ahmed & Sons (MAS) Private Limited has been a cornerstone of construction and engineering excellence in Gilgit-Baltistan for over 50 years. As a PEC registered firm (Category C-3), we have built our reputation on delivering quality infrastructure projects that stand the test of time in some of the most challenging terrains in the world.

  • PEC Registered Construction Company (C-3 Category)
  • 50+ Years of Industry Experience
  • Specialized in High-Altitude Construction Projects

From road construction and traffic engineering to building construction and renewable energy solutions, we bring expertise, dedication, and innovation to every project. Our client-centric approach and commitment to quality have made us a trusted partner for government and private sector projects alike.
