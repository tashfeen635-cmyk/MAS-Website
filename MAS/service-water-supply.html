Water Supply Solutions

We provide comprehensive water supply and civil engineering solutions, bringing clean drinking water to communities across Gilgit-Baltistan through modern infrastructure and sustainable practices.

Civil Engineering & Water Supply Services

Maqsood Ahmed & Sons has been instrumental in providing clean water access to communities across Gilgit-Baltistan for over 50 years. Our civil engineering division specializes in designing and constructing water supply systems that are efficient, sustainable, and tailored to the unique geographical challenges of the region.

We understand the critical importance of clean water for community health and development. Our team of civil engineers and water specialists work diligently to ensure that every project delivers safe, reliable water supply to homes, businesses, and public facilities.

Our Water Supply Services Include:

  • Water distribution network design and construction
  • Pipeline installation and maintenance
  • Water reservoir and storage tank construction
  • Pumping station installation
  • Water treatment facilities
  • Sewerage and sanitation systems
  • Irrigation channel construction

Key Projects:

  • Water supply Scheme to Danyore and Sultanabad in 1989
  • Water supply and installation of pumping station at District H.Q Ghakuch in 2002
  • Greater Water Supply Scheme in 2004
  • Reparing Of Pipe line in Gilgit main City

Our approach combines modern engineering techniques with local knowledge to deliver water supply solutions that work effectively in the mountainous terrain of Gilgit-Baltistan. We prioritize sustainability, using gravity-fed systems where possible and ensuring minimal environmental impact. Every project is completed to the highest standards, ensuring long-term reliability and community benefit.
