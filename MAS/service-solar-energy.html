Solar Energy Solutions

Maqsood Ahmed & Sons is committed to bringing clean, renewable energy to Gilgit-Baltistan. Our solar energy division specializes in supplying, designing, installing, and maintaining solar power systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

We understand the unique energy challenges faced by communities in mountainous regions, where grid connectivity can be unreliable. Our solar solutions provide energy independence, reducing reliance on expensive generators and fossil fuels while contributing to environmental conservation.

Our Solar Energy Services Include:

  • Solar Panel Supply
  • System Design & Engineering
  • Installation Services
  • Maintenance & Support
  • Performance Optimization
  • Battery Storage Solutions
  • Off-grid & Grid-tied Systems
  • Energy Audits & Consultancy

Key Service Areas:

  • Solar Panel Supply - High-quality solar panels from trusted manufacturers for all applications
  • System Design & Engineering - Custom solar system design tailored to your specific energy needs
  • Installation Services - Professional installation by trained technicians ensuring optimal performance
  • Maintenance & Support - Comprehensive maintenance and after-sales support services
  • Performance Optimization - System monitoring and optimization for maximum energy output

We use high-quality solar panels and components from trusted manufacturers, ensuring optimal performance and longevity. Our team of trained technicians provides professional installation and ongoing maintenance services. We also offer comprehensive warranties and after-sales support to ensure your solar investment delivers maximum returns for years to come.
