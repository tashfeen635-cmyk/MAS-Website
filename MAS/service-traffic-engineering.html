Traffic Engineering Solutions

We provide comprehensive traffic engineering services to enhance road safety and traffic flow across Gilgit-Baltistan's challenging mountain roads, ensuring safer journeys for all travelers.

Professional Traffic Engineering Services

Maqsood Ahmed & Sons provides comprehensive traffic engineering solutions designed to improve road safety and traffic management across Gilgit-Baltistan. With over 50 years of experience working on mountain roads and highways, we understand the unique challenges of traffic management in high-altitude terrain.

Our traffic engineering team combines technical expertise with local knowledge to design and implement solutions that make roads safer for vehicles, pedestrians, and cyclists. We work closely with government agencies and local authorities to ensure compliance with national and international safety standards.

Our Traffic Engineering Services Include:

  • Traffic flow analysis and planning
  • Road signage design and installation
  • Road marking and line painting
  • Safety barrier installation
  • Traffic signal systems
  • Speed control measures
  • Pedestrian crossing facilities
  • Road safety audits

Key Projects:

  • Ghizer Road Safety Improvements
  • Urban Traffic Management Systems
  • Mountain Pass Safety Signage
  • School Zone Safety Installations

Safety is our top priority. We use high-quality materials and proven techniques to ensure that all traffic engineering installations are durable and effective. Our solutions are designed to withstand the harsh weather conditions of Gilgit-Baltistan while providing clear guidance and protection for road users. From busy urban intersections to remote mountain passes, we deliver traffic engineering solutions that save lives.
