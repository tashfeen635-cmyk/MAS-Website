About Us - Over 50 Years of Excellence

M/S Maqsood Ahmed & Sons started its small scale operations humbly in 1972 and today it is one of the leading construction companies of the GB region. This has become possible only with determined efforts of our dedicated staff and encouragement from our valued clients.

Hassle-Free Projects

MAS was formed to deliver hassle-free projects in timely and cost-effective manners. We have no claims or litigation with any of our clients.

Ready for Challenges
