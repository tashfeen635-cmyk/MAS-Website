Building Construction Excellence

We construct durable and aesthetically pleasing buildings designed to withstand the unique climate conditions of Gilgit-Baltistan, combining modern architecture with local expertise.

Professional Building Construction Services

Maqsood Ahmed & Sons has been constructing quality buildings across Gilgit-Baltistan for over 50 years. Our building construction services encompass residential homes, commercial complexes, institutional buildings, and industrial facilities. We understand the unique challenges of construction in mountainous regions and design structures that are both functional and resilient.

Our experienced team of architects, engineers, and construction workers ensures that every building project meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and aesthetics. We use premium materials and modern construction techniques to deliver structures that stand the test of time.

Our Building Construction Services Include:

  • Residential house construction
  • Commercial building development
  • Institutional facilities (schools, hospitals, offices)
  • Industrial warehouses and factories
  • Renovation and remodeling
  • Foundation and structural work

Key Projects:

  • Extension Of KG Girls High School Gilgit in 1982
  • Sheds Erection Of Transfermor Lines in 1989
  • Construction of Directorate of Agriculture in 1992
  • Construction of HDC hospital at Jutial Gilgit in 2002
  • Upgradation of Girls Primary School kashroot to Middle Standard

We prioritize client satisfaction and work closely with our customers to understand their vision and requirements. From initial design to final handover, we maintain transparent communication and deliver projects on time and within budget. Our commitment to quality and safety has made us a trusted name in building construction throughout the region.
